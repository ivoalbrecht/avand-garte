Avand-Garte: classify your painting

Welcome!

Avand-Garte is an online tool that classifies artworks based on their genre

It is a free service that people can use to label their artwork.

Let's Begin

The tool

Upload your image from your pc with the button below. Accepted formats are .jpg and .png. The prediction takes a while.

About

This project has been carried out as a graduation project for the data science course of Spiced Academy. This project has been conducted by Ivo Albrecht.

The images used for training the model and the website originate from the WikiArt public database.
